COVID-19 Screening Tool

This test was elaborated by doctors from different specialties and different Lebanese medical schools, in collaboration with the Lebanese Society of Critical Care Medicine, the Lebanese Pulmonary Society, and the Lebanese Society of Anesthesiologists.


The objective of this test to evaluate your risk of having contracted the COVID-19 coronavirus.
At the end of this test you will receive the recommendation according to your answers.
The use of this test and its content does not replace the necessary advice given by your physician.
The information made available within the framework of the application serves only as first level information.
Any decision made by the user must be made autonomously on the basis of relevant scientific and clinical information.
If in doubt or for any more information, do not hesitate to contact your physician.
